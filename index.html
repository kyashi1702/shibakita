How many BNB can I claim?

You can claim the amount of the BNB based on the ratio of SHIBAKITA you hold/the total BNB pool. For example, you hold 1% of SHIBAKITA, you can withdraw 1% of the BNB reward pool.

What is BNB pool?

4% of every transaction is taken and re-distributed to all SHIBAKITA INU holders in $BNB. This amount of BNB called the BNB pool.

What if I don't claim BNB in my lifecycle?

If you don’t claim at your cycle and other holders claim, the BNB pool will reduce. So then the ratio of your SHIBAKITA/BNB pool reduces as well. That’s why you should claim as soon as your cycle is ready
